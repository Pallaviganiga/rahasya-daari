About Us

We are dedicated in organizing trips, treks, excursions, corporate packages, family trips, student trip packages across India.

We also customize the trips, treks as per your requirements.

If you are solo, couple, family or friends, join our weekend treks and tours from Bengaluru which are organized every weekend to experience the best weekend of your life and be a part of the Rahasya Daari family.

Vision

  • To reaffirm the bond between the Nature and mankind by unravelling the knots of materialistic routine.

  • Let us be your guide to help you embrace the serenity of mother Nature.

  • To be able to connect the beautiful nature from different part of the world to you.

Mission

  • To make all possible ways to make Nature close to people's heart and present memories for a lifetime.

  • To lead community in protecting and promoting the divinity of Nature and Bio-diversity.
